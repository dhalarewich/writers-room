# The repair guarantee test

I broke a $340 jacket on purpose last month.

Sliced the pit zip clean through with a seam ripper, sent it back to the maker with an honest note, and started a timer. This is the third brand I've done this to. The first one sent a form email and a discount code. The second quoted me $45 and six weeks.

This one called me. A human, two days later, asked what I'd been doing when it tore, talked me through their patch options, and had the jacket back on my porch in eleven days, repaired with a contrasting panel and a note that said "we log every failure by seam, thanks for the data point."

That jacket company will get every dollar I spend in this category for the next decade, and I will tell this story at every trailhead.

The repair desk is the brand. The rest is fabric.

# Eight points or nothing

Pitched on a rock ledge above Cataract Creek and paid for it by 1am.

The Halcyon wants eight honest stake points. I gave it five and a prayer, and the wind found the slack fly like it had a map. Spent an hour re-rigging guylines to boulders in the dark.

The tent was never the problem. The site was. Gear reviews keep grading shelters on fabric while the failure lives in the geometry: five points is a different, worse tent than eight, whatever the label says.

Count your points before you count your grams.

# Warranty economics at a cottage brand

A pit-zip replacement is 20–35 minutes of bench time. At the loaded labor rate we used ($38/hr in 2023) that is roughly a $22 repair before parts and shipping; the zipper itself is under $4 in coil and sliders. Round-trip shipping is the real cost driver: $9–14 each way domestic.

The counterintuitive part: a free repair on a $340 jacket costs the brand about $50 and produces a customer whose repeat-purchase rate we measured at 2.7× baseline. The discount-code path (our old policy) cost $34 in margin and produced no measurable loyalty lift.

Failure logging by seam is what makes repairs compound: three failures on the same seam in a quarter flagged a pattern that a $0.30 bartack fixed at the factory. See [[failure-modes]].

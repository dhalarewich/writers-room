# Shelter failure modes

From the Halcyon failure log, 2019–2024, ~3,100 tickets:

1. Zipper coil wear — 41% of tickets, and roughly two-thirds of those are grit-related (user-preventable with a toothbrush).
2. Pole-sleeve abrasion at the ferrule — 18%.
3. Fly delamination at seam tape — 14%, almost always storage-related (packed wet).
4. Stake-out point tears — 11%, correlated with rock-ledge pitching.
5. Mesh panel punctures — 7%, dogs and cactus in equal measure.

The repair desk's rule of thumb: if a failure shows up before night 30, it's a defect; after night 100, it's a maintenance story. The 30–100 band is where reviews earn their keep. See [[warranty-economics]].

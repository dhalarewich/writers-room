# Banned patterns

The ceilings above are enforced by `wr sweep` and judged by the Warden. The four structural tells it counts:

1. **Contrast-snaps** — the "not X, it's Y" family in all its variants. Budget is in the ceilings block, and the survivors must be deliberate payoff lines (hook or close), never filler.
2. **Em dashes** — ceiling per channel class above. Rewrite overflow with commas, periods, parentheses, or a colon.
3. **Rule-of-three** — three tidy fragments or four parallel nouns read as a list even without bullets. Dissolve into a flowing sentence or trace one consequence. Never fix a four-item list by trimming it to three.
4. **Metronome rhythm** — if every sentence is the same medium-punchy length, the cadence itself is the tell. Keep at least one longer breath sentence.
